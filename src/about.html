About us

We are a modern marketing organization that works to develop both domestic and incoming tourism. We are professionals who provide information about Prague to both domestic and incoming tourists and the media. We develop and implement the citys destination marketing concept.


It reflects the quality of life of Praguers, the local community, and the development of cultivated tourism. We organize educational lecture series on the past and present of Prague for professionals and the general public. We are responsible for managing the operations of the historical Old Town Hall. We are members of the international organization ECM (European Cities Marketing) and are also active in the Association of Tourist Information Centers of the Czech Republic.


We work with other organizations such as CzechTourism, Czech Centers, the Ministry of Regional Development of the Czech Republic, and Czech embassies and consulates that fall under the auspices of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Czech Republic.
